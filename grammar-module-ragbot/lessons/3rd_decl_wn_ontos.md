### Introduction to 3rd Declension (ὄντος)
The 3rd declension in Greek is a group of nouns that follow a specific pattern of endings to indicate their grammatical case. This declension is crucial for reading and writing in Greek, as it includes a wide range of nouns that are used in various contexts. Understanding the 3rd declension will significantly improve your reading comprehension and writing skills in Greek.
